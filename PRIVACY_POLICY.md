# Privacy Policy for OneRep

**Last updated: April 15, 2026**

OneRep is a strength tracking application developed as an independent project. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use OneRep, the following data is collected:

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Email address** | Account creation and authentication |
| **Password** (hashed, never stored in plain text) | Account authentication via Supabase Auth |
| **Workout data** (sessions, sets, reps, weights, timestamps) | Core app functionality and cross-device sync |
| **Personal bests** (exercise, weight, reps, date achieved) | Tracking your performance records |
| **Workout routines and splits** | Saving and restoring your training programmes |
| **Badges earned** (achievement keys and dates) | Tracking your in-app achievements |
| **Custom exercises** (name, body part, equipment type) | User-created exercise library entries |

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, payment information, photos, contacts, or any device identifiers.

---

## 2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used exclusively to provide the functionality of the app:

- Authenticating your account
- Syncing your workout data across devices
- Restoring your data when you sign in on a new device
- Displaying your personal bests and progress history

Your data is **never used for advertising, profiling, analytics, or sold to third parties.**

---

## 3. Data Storage and Third Parties

OneRep uses **Supabase** (supabase.com) as its backend infrastructure for authentication and data storage. Supabase processes your data on our behalf under their data processing agreements.

- Supabase Privacy Policy: [https://supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- Your workout data is stored in Supabase's cloud infrastructure and is associated with your user account.
- No other third-party services receive your data.

---

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained for as long as your account exists.
- When you sign out of the app, all locally stored data is deleted from your device.
- To request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the email address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

---

## 5. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

As a user based in the United Kingdom or European Economic Area, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** processing of your data
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Object** to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address in Section 7.

---

## 6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data:

- Passwords are managed by Supabase Auth and are never stored in plain text
- Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
- Access to your data in Supabase is restricted by Row-Level Security policies tied to your authenticated user ID

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, sign out immediately and contact us.
